Lead the reader through it

By default visitors browse your media freely. Write one Section and the front door becomes a guided walk-through — no setting, just a consequence. Add one and watch:

Notice · You never picked a layout — writing a narrative (or not) chose it for you. model.ts — sections.length > 0 ? 'narrative' : 'grid'

What one Section is

A Section is a single beat — your prose plus a framed view of one Object (you set the "camera" right on the canvas). An ordered run of beats is the narrative.

Authored on the canvas You frame a Section by drawing the region (or marking the time-range) you want the reader to land on — so what you see while authoring is exactly what they'll see. A Section is independent of your Notes: it's its own beat, not a tour of existing markers.
Try it In an object, add a beat, write a line of prose, and frame a detail. Open the Viewer — your exhibit now greets readers with that walk-through.
